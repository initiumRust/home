Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server? ▼
Make sure you have Rust installed, then search for Initium in the server browser or use the following command: press F1 and type: connect initiumhk.ddns.net
When does the servers wipe? ▼
In Rust servers, wipes can happen weekly, biweekly, or monthly, depending on the server's configuration. The exact wipe schedule is usually shared in the server's announcements.
Are there any server rules? ▼
Yes, we want everyone to enjoy their time here. You can find the full list of rules in our discord server. Key highlights include: No cheating or exploiting. Respect other players and admins.
Who do I contact for help?? ▼
If you encounter any issues, you can report them via ticket system, which can be found in our discord server!
Can I suggest features or changes? ▼
Absolutely! We value community input. You can find a special channel made especially for submitting your suggestions! Which can be found in our discord server!
What regions do you support? ▼
Currently we support only EU [EUROPE] region, but in the feature we plan to expand which will include AU [AUSTRALIA] and US [UNITED STATES]
The server restarted, and i experienced a rollback. Why? ▼
During server restarts or crashes, player progression may be subject to a rollback. Initium Rust servers has implemented measures to minimize the impact of rollbacks and prevent them whenever possible. However, if a rollback does occur, it cannot be undone, and it will affect all players equally.
I got banned, is there a way to appeal? ▼
Yes! There still is a chance to get unbanned! If you think you got false banned Please head over to our discord server and open a ban appeal ticket if you think you were wrongfully banned!
What should i do if i find a bug? ▼
If you encounter an in-game bug, please join our Discord server and submit a support ticket [Bug Report] to report the issue to our staff team. There is a chance that you will be rewarded!
Where can i find updates about servers? ▼
You can find everything in our discord server! Including changelogs, wipe alerts, map votes, polls and announcements!
Is there a way to become a staff? ▼
For now, we don't look for more staff members, but in future we plan to open applications. Join our discord to stay updated!
Does the server host events? ▼
For now, we don't host any events, but in future we plan to host events. Join our discord to stay updated!
